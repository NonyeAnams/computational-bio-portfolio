## Selected Projects

**1. 🧬 Breast Cancer Survival Analysis (TCGA-BRCA)**

End-to-end RNA-seq pipeline integrating differential expression, pathway enrichment (GSEA/ORA), survival modeling, and machine learning across 1,019 breast cancer patients.

> Key contribution: Identified prognostic transcriptional signatures and demonstrated improved predictive performance using clinico-genomic integration.

🔗 https://github.com/nonyeanams/brca-transcriptomic-ml-pipeline

**2. 🧬 DLBCL Transcriptomic & Survival Modeling (GEO GSE10846)**

Microarray-based analysis of 200 DLBCL patients integrating subtype-specific differential expression, pathway dysregulation, and Cox proportional hazards modeling.

> Key contribution: Revealed subtype-dependent survival architecture and validated molecular subtype as a dominant prognostic factor.

🔗 https://github.com/nonyeanams/dlbcl-transcriptomic-analysis

**3. 🧬 Pathway-Centric Chemotherapy Response Modeling**

Confounding-aware RNA-seq analysis of breast cancer treatment response combining batch correction, ssGSEA pathway scoring, and predictive machine learning.

> Key contribution: Demonstrated robustness of pathway-level representations under technical and biological confounding.

🔗 https://github.com/nonyeanams/breast-cancer-pathway-ml

**4. 🧬 Single-Cell Immune–Fibroblast Interaction Analysis**

Single-cell RNA-seq analysis of biomaterial-induced immune response, resolving macrophage and fibroblast states and identifying candidate intercellular signaling axes.

> Key contribution: Mapped immune–stromal interaction programs associated with fibrotic remodeling.

🔗 https://github.com/nonyeanams/immune-material-FBR
